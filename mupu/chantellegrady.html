Bio/contact

Chantelle is a lifestyle, food and travel photographer from Australia, currently living in Berkeley California. Previous to her work as a photographer she was a designer for a number of magazines, and an art director for a few fashion labels. Her passion for editorial led her to pursue work as a photographer.

Working with natural light she brings a soft and refined feel to her work, aiming to capture an emotion and visually tell a story. Chantelle has worked internationally for various clients, and in 2013 co-produced her first book called Dévorer Montréal.

In her spare time she runs a blog called The Grounded Kitchen, a website with a focus on wholesome recipes.
